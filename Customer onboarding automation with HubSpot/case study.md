# Customer Onboarding Automation System

## Overview

This project automates the customer onboarding journey from the moment a customer signs up through their first week of engagement.

The system combines CRM management, automated email sequences, milestone tracking, and team notifications to create a consistent onboarding experience while reducing manual administrative work.

By automating key touchpoints, businesses can improve customer engagement, increase retention, and ensure every new customer receives a structured onboarding experience.

---

## The Problem

Many businesses struggle to deliver a consistent onboarding experience for new customers.

Common challenges include:

* Delayed welcome communications
* Manual CRM updates
* Missed follow-ups
* Inconsistent onboarding processes
* Lack of visibility for internal teams
* Customers becoming disengaged during their first few days

As customer volume grows, maintaining a high-quality onboarding experience becomes increasingly difficult.

---

## The Solution

I designed an automated onboarding workflow that guides customers through a structured onboarding journey while keeping internal teams informed at every stage.

The workflow automatically:

* Receives customer signup information
* Validates customer data
* Creates CRM records
* Sends personalized onboarding emails
* Delivers onboarding resources
* Schedules follow-up communications
* Updates customer status in HubSpot
* Notifies internal teams throughout the process

This ensures every customer receives timely communication and support without requiring manual intervention.
