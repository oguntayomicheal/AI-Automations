# AI-Powered Real Estate Lead Matching & CRM Automation

## Overview

This project automates the process of qualifying real estate leads, matching prospects to suitable property listings, updating CRM records, and initiating personalized follow-up communication.

The workflow combines AI-powered lead analysis with CRM automation to reduce manual work, improve response times, and create a better customer experience.

---

## The Problem

Real estate brokers receive inquiries from multiple channels and often spend significant time performing repetitive tasks such as:

* Reviewing customer requirements
* Qualifying prospects
* Searching available listings
* Updating CRM records
* Sending follow-up emails
* Scheduling consultations

These manual processes slow down response times and limit the number of leads a team can effectively manage.

---

## The Solution

I built an intelligent automation workflow that:

1. Captures customer inquiries from a form.
2. Uses AI to analyze customer preferences and requirements.
3. Matches customer criteria against available property listings.
4. Scores and ranks the most relevant options.
5. Creates or updates records in Zoho CRM.
6. Sends personalized follow-up emails.
7. Provides a streamlined handoff to the sales team.

The result is a faster, more scalable lead qualification process.

---

## Workflow Architecture

Customer Inquiry

↓

* Property type

### AI-Powered Property Matching
