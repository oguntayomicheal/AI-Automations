# AI-Powered Lead Generation & Personalized Outreach Automation

## Overview

This project automates the process of discovering business leads, enriching contact information, generating personalized outreach messages, and sending cold emails at scale.

Designed for digital marketing agencies, sales teams, and business development professionals, the workflow eliminates hours of manual prospecting and outreach work by combining web scraping, AI-powered lead enrichment, and automated email generation.

---

## The Problem

Finding qualified business leads is often a time-consuming and repetitive process.

Sales and marketing teams typically spend hours:

* Searching for businesses manually
* Collecting company information
* Visiting websites to find contact emails
* Organizing lead data in spreadsheets
* Writing personalized outreach emails
* Sending and tracking communications

As lead volume grows, these manual tasks become difficult to scale and reduce the time available for higher-value sales activities.

---

## The Solution

I developed an AI-powered lead generation and outreach system that automates the entire prospecting workflow.

The system accepts a business category and location, discovers relevant companies, enriches lead data, extracts contact information using AI, generates personalized cold emails, and automatically sends outreach messages.

The result is a streamlined lead acquisition process that significantly reduces manual effort while maintaining personalized communication.

## Key Features

### Automated Business Discovery

Identifies businesses based on:

* Industry
* Service category
* Geographic location
* User-defined lead volume

---

### AI-Powered Contact Enrichment

Uses AI to analyze company websites and identify the most relevant contact email addresses for outreach.

This removes the need for manual website reviews and contact research.

---

### Lead Database Management

Automatically stores lead information including:

* Company name
* Website
* Phone number
* Business category
* Address
* Contact email
* Outreach status

---

### Personalized Outreach Generation

Generates customized cold emails based on selected communication styles such as:

* Friendly
* Professional
* Simple

Each message is tailored to improve engagement and relevance.

---

### Automated Email Delivery

Sends outreach emails directly through connected email accounts while maintaining tracking records and delivery status.
